About me

Currently employed as a software developer providing messaging solutions using various web and desktop applications. Primarily working in C#, ASP.Net, JavaScript, and whatever procedural language the current database(s) uses . However, learning new languages (shallow or deep) is always fun and exciting (like my current exploration in to LISP).

My "bible" would be the Pragmatic Programmer which has forged how I approach every problem. Interesting factoid though is a lot of it can be applied just as well to real life outside of the computer-verse. Actually, periodic browsing of the book has helped to refresh its wisdom within my work and so I find myself continuously striving to perfect certain techniques.
